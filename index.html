Section Title

Nepal is a small landlocked country in South Asia, located between India and China. It has a diverse geography that ranges from the high Himalayan mountains in the north to the fertile
Terai plains in the south. Mount Everest, the highest mountain in the world, lies on the Nepal-China border.

Aside Title

Nepal has a population of around 29 million people and is known for its cultural and religious diversity.
The official language of Nepal is Nepali, and there are over 100 other spoken languages in the country.
